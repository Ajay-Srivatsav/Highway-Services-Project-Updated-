TOWING SERVICES




When on-site repairs are not possible or if your vehicle is immobilized due to severe damage,
you can rely on us to get your vehicle safely transported to the nearest authorized workshop,
garage or service station. Our qualified technicians with help your vehicle be towed in our reliable
flat-bed tow truck. As per your membership plan, you can avail free towing services up to the distance mentioned,and thereon as per charge mentioned.

BATTERY JUMPSTART




A dead battery in the middle of the road can keep you stranded for long hours. Our 24-hour roadside assistance service helps you get going. Our trained technicians know exactly how to start your vehicle without causing any damage.

FLAT TYRE




We ensure that your vehicle’s flat tyre is repaired or replaced right away – either on-site or at the nearest garage, so that you can get back on the road in as little time as possible. Our authorised team will reach your location in less than an hour, depending on the location, to fix the punctured tyre(in case of multiple flat tyres) or replace it with the spare tyre.

LOST or LOCKED KEYS




If you lose keys to your vehicle or accidentally leave them inside, do not panic! Simply give us a call on our toll-free number and we will immediately help you get access to your vehicle keys. Our team will either get the spare keys from your home or tow the vehicle to an authorized garage, service station or workshop near the vicinity. Our technicians always ensure that your car is unlocked with safe, precautionary measures and guaranty zero damage.

FUEL PROBLEMS




If you get stuck because of wrong fuelling or low fuel, our team will be at your location. We help you with sufficient fuel to continue your journey and reach the nearest gas station or rectify wrong fuelling at the nearest authorised garage. You may also avail a cab service as per your membership plan. If you’re an existing member, we offer free fuel assistance service up to a maximum of 5 litres basis your plan.
